The Cottage Garden

The cottage garden is a distinct style of garden that uses an informal design, dense plantings and a mixtureof ornamental and edible plants.

The Cottage Garden originated in England and its history can be traced back for centuris, although they were re-invented in 1870's England, when stylized versions wre formed as a reaction the more structured and rigorously maintained English estate gardens.

The earliest cottage gardens were more practical than their modern descendants, with an amphasis on vegetables and gerb, along with some fruit trees.
