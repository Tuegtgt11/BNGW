From Garden to Plate

A potageris a French term for an ornamental vegetable of kitchen garden. Often folowers (editble and non-editble) and herbs are planted with the vegetables to enhance the garden's beauty. The goal is to make the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for their color and form. Many are traind to grow uoward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise their funtion of providing for a home in a wide array of forms-from the carefree stule of the cottage garden to the formality of a knot garden.
